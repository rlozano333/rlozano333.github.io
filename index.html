Strategic Planning at Open El Paso

El Paso is a reflective and future-focused community. As a key element of the City's Strategic Planning Process, the Mayor, City Council and Senior Leaders convened a session on May 16, 2019. City leaders reviewed work accomplished and traction made in the eight adopted strategic goals activated in 2015 and the 20 in 2020 visionary initiatives geared toward vision attainment.

During this Strategic Planning Session, Mayor and Council adopted a refreshed Mission and Vision and approved the new 25 by 25 visionary initiatives setting the tone and direction of the City's service delivery.


Mission

Deliver exceptional services to support a high quality of life and place for our community.

Vision         

Develop a vibrant regional economy, safe and beautiful neighborhoods and exceptional recreational, cultural and educational opportunities powered by a high performing government.

Values        

Integrity, Respect, Excellence, Accountability, People

The City of El Paso's mission guides us forward as the bedrock of our our purpose-exceptional services. This facilitates the community coming together and developing sound strategies, that will ensure stability throughout any inevitable future challenges.

Our vision drives us to inspire one another to work towards an incredible future for the entire area, and for the generations to come. We understand where we are headed and what needs to be achieved in order to accomplish those goals.

The core beliefs of integrity, respect, excellence, and accountability have resulted in the city making great strides in quality of life, cultural, recreational and educational opportunities. Our sound leadership team has guided us toward financial stability by eliminating a $7.8 million deficit! Reflections on lessons learned from the past, while strategically preparing for the future, creates excitement for what lay ahead! We all look forward to developing an exceptiona community for all people of El Paso.
