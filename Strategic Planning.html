 open el paso Strategic Goal progress  

Welcome to the Open El Paso Dashboard! This service is designed to help the community keep track of how we are performing as an organization and explain the progress towards the strategic goals of the city.

Updating and monitoring this infomation ensures that we work toward our Vision. Developing a vibrant regional economy, safe and beautiful neighborhoods, exceptional recreational, cultural and educational opportunities, powered by a high performing government.
